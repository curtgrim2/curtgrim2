Website Description
Note: All elements including background images were created by me, Curtis Grimsley II
In building this website, the main thing that I kept in mind was simplicity. Users from all ages with a basic understanding of English should be able to navigate through this website with plenty of ease.
Sometimes less is more and I am of the mindset that an exhaustion of too many resources is unnecessary and in some cases too much for people to handle. I took the minamalist approach with planning and constructing this website so that I wouldn't bombard anyone who comes across any page. The theme that the site is based around is the unknown realms of outer space. I thought this was appropriate with this being seen as my own personal space where I can express myself and showcase my abilities.
